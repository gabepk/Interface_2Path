How to search a pathway?

  1. In the first field, choose an organism or leave "all organisms". It is possible to obtain the list of organisms by entering the first letters of their name.
  2. In the second field choose the source compound. It is possible to obtain the list of compounds by entering the first letters of their name.
  3. In the third field choose the target compound. It is possible to obtain the list of compounds by entering the first letters of their name.
  4. If there is a terpenoid metabolic pathway between the two compounds for the chosen organism, 2Path will show the result in a graph.
  5. Click in Search Now

How to explore 2Path data?

  1. In the first field, choose an organism. It is possible to obtain the list of organisms by entering the first letters of their name.
  2. Click in Search Now
  3. Using the datagrid, it is possible to use the pagination and change the order.
